Drop institution/company logo images in this folder, for example:

  fms.png            (education.md - FMS)
  snu.png             (education.md - Shiv Nadar)
  spv.png              (education.md - Sardar Patel Vidyalaya)
  hero-fincorp.png    (experience.md - Hero Fincorp)
  hul.png              (experience.md - HUL)
  bain.png              (experience.md - Bain)
  dell.png               (experience.md - Dell)
  icgeb.png                (experience.md - ICGEB)

Then in education.md / experience.md, replace the placeholder line, e.g.:
  <span class="org-badge">FMS</span>
with:
  <img src="{{ '/assets/img/logos/fms.png' | relative_url }}" class="org-badge badge-img" alt="FMS logo">

Tip: square logos (or logos on a transparent/white background) look best —
the badge is a 34x34px rounded square with a small padding.

Delete this file once you've added your logos.
